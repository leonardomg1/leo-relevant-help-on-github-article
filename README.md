## Why this article exists

Many people want to help open source, but do not know where to begin.

This article shows a simple path: start with **small, useful contributions** such as translating a README, improving documentation, fixing unclear instructions, reviewing links, or helping people find beginner-friendly issues.

It treats open source contribution as **digital philanthropy**: a way to give time, attention, clarity, and access to people you may never meet.
